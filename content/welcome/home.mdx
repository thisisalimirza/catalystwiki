Welcome to the **Catalyst Fellow Wiki** — your one-stop guide for everything you need to build a healthcare solution this summer, regardless of your technical background.

<Callout type="success" title="You belong here">
Most Catalyst fellows come from clinical, research, or policy backgrounds — not software engineering. This wiki is written for you. Every guide assumes you're starting from zero on the technical side.
</Callout>

## What is Catalyst?

Catalyst is a 10-week summer fellowship for healthcare professionals who want to build technology solutions to real clinical problems. You'll be matched with a sponsored problem (or bring your own), mentored by industry experts, and supported with real AI credits and tools to build a working MVP.

At the end: **Demo Day** — a live pitch to sponsors, investors, and the broader MDplus community.

## The one-sentence version

Your job this summer: **find a real problem, understand who has it, and build the simplest possible solution that demonstrates it can work** — then show the world on Demo Day.

The rest of this wiki will show you how.
