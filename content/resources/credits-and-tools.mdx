As a Catalyst fellow, you have access to a substantial set of free tools and credits. This page is the master list — bookmark it.

## AI Credits

### Anthropic (Claude)
**$30,000 in API credits** — Managed by MDplus on behalf of all Catalyst teams.

- **To get your API key:** Email catalyst@mdplus.community with your team name
- **Model to use:** `claude-sonnet-4-6` for most tasks; `claude-haiku-4-5` for high-volume or speed-critical tasks
- **Console:** [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com) — Arvind can give your team read access to track usage
- **Documentation:** [docs.anthropic.com](https://docs.anthropic.com)

$30,000 is a lot of credits. For context: processing 1,000 words of clinical notes with Claude Sonnet costs roughly $0.003. You would need to run approximately 10 million words of clinical text through the API to exhaust the full allocation. Build without worrying about cost.

### Google HAI-DEF (MedGemma)

Google's foundation models for health care and life sciences are open and free, available for both research and commercial purposes under the HAI-DEF license. [Read more at goo.gle/hai-def](https://goo.gle/hai-def).

**Free access to MedGemma** via Google Cloud Vertex AI, courtesy of Google HAI-DEF (Health AI Developer Foundations), one of our key program sponsors.

- **To get access:** Email catalyst@mdplus.community with "MedGemma access request — [Team name]"
- **Alternative free access:** Hugging Face Inference API (no credentials needed, limited rate)
- **Documentation:** [cloud.google.com/vertex-ai](https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai)
- **Questions:** Post in **#google-hidef** in Slack or request an office hours slot

## Data & Research

### Synthetic patient data
- **Synthea** — Open-source synthetic patient generator. Free. [synthetichealth.github.io/synthea](https://synthetichealth.github.io/synthea)
- **Claude** — Ask it to generate realistic de-identified clinical scenarios
- **Mockaroo** — Custom fake dataset generator. Free tier.

### Clinical information
- **PubMed** — Free access to medical literature
- **UpToDate** — Check if your institution provides access
- **ClinicalTrials.gov** — Trial data, free
- **CDC Wonder** — Public health data, free
- **AHRQ Data Tools** — Healthcare quality data, free
